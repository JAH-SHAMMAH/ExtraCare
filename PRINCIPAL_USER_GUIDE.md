# Principal User Guide — Fairview School Portal

**For:** Principal / School Administrator (role: Administrator). You have full
access to the portal.

---

## 1. Logging in
1. Open the portal in your browser.
2. Enter your **@fairviewschoolng.com** email and password.
3. Select **Sign in**. There is no organisation field — this portal is for
   Fairview School only.

> Only registered Fairview accounts can sign in. Non-school email addresses are
> rejected. After 5 failed attempts an account locks — reset it from **Users**.

## 2. Student management
- **Sidebar → Education → Students.**
- **Add a student:** *New Student* → fill profile (name, ID, date of birth,
  guardian details) → assign a class.
- **Link a parent:** open the student → add a guardian (links the parent's
  account so they can monitor that child and receive attendance alerts).
- **Bulk import:** *Students → Import* → upload a CSV (the wizard maps columns
  and validates before saving).
- Manage **Classes, Subjects, Teachers, and Staff** from the same Education menu.

## 3. Attendance
- **Daily marking:** Education → **Attendance** (Tracker) to mark a class.
- **Insights:** Education → **Attendance Insights** —
  - Choose a **date** and optional **class**.
  - See **Present / Late / Absent / Excused** totals and the attendance rate.
  - Review the **Late arrivals** and **Absent / not checked in** panels.
  - Scroll the full **roster** with check-in/out times.
  - The month-to-date summary shows school-wide totals.
- Attendance can be recorded manually by staff/teachers; each check-in/out
  **notifies the child's parents instantly**.

## 4. Finance
- **Education → Fee Management** to set up and track fees.
- The payments module records parent payments (Paystack) and lets you, as
  administrator, **reconcile** transactions, view the **transactions** list,
  pull **receipts**, and see the **outstanding** summary.
- Parents pay online and you confirm/reconcile here.

## 5. HR
- **Sidebar → HR Manager** — staff profiles, **birthdays**, and **events**.
- **Leave Admin** — review and **approve/reject** staff leave applications.
- **My HRM Info** / **My Leave** are your own personal records.

## 6. Reports
- **Report Cards** — generate per-student, per-term report cards.
- **Analytics** — school overview (students, attendance, activity).
- **Audit Log** — an immutable record of logins, attendance, and changes for
  security and compliance.

## 7. Tips
- Keep guardian links up to date — they drive parent visibility and alerts.
- Use the role that fits each user (Administrator, Management, Teacher, Staff,
  Parent, Student) so people see only what they need.
- Rotate any default/initial passwords immediately after creating accounts.
