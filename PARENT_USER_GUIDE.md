# Parent User Guide — Fairview School Portal

**For:** Parents and Guardians.

---

## 1. Logging in
- Open the portal and sign in with the **@fairviewschoolng.com** email the
  school created for you, plus your password.
- Accounts are **by invitation** — if you don't have one, contact the school
  office. You will only ever see your own children.

## 2. Attendance notifications
- The moment your child checks in or out of school, you get an alert:
  - *"Ferdinand has successfully checked into Fairview School at 7:32 AM."*
  - *"Ferdinand has successfully checked out of Fairview School at 3:15 PM."*
- See them on **My Children → Attendance** (the *Attendance alerts* panel).
  Use **Mark all read** to clear them.
- Alerts appear in the portal in near real time.

## 3. Monitoring your child
- **Sidebar → My Children** shows each child with their attendance %, pending
  assignments, and latest grade.
- **My Children → Attendance** shows, per child:
  - This month's **Present / Late / Absent / Excused** totals and days logged.
  - The recent **check-in / check-out** history with times.
  - The live **attendance alerts** feed.
- Switch between children using the selector at the top.

## 4. Results
- From **My Children**, open a child → **Grades & reports** to view grades and
  download report cards.

## 5. Fees
- From your child's record you can view **outstanding fees** and **pay online**
  (Paystack). You can look up by student and **verify** a completed payment.
- Receipts and balances update after a successful payment.

## 6. Privacy & safety
- You can only access **your own children's** information; the portal blocks any
  attempt to view another family's child.
- Keep your password private. If you suspect a problem, contact the school
  office to reset it.

## 7. Tips
- Enable browser notifications if you'd like to spot new alerts quickly.
- If you don't see a child you expect, the school may not have linked your
  account yet — contact the office.
