# Teacher User Guide — Fairview School Portal

**For:** Teachers and Class Teachers.

---

## 1. Logging in
- Open the portal and sign in with your **@fairviewschoolng.com** email and
  password. If you don't have an account, contact the school office (there is no
  public sign-up).

## 2. Attendance
- **Sidebar → Class Attendance.**
- Pick one of **your classes** and the **date**.
- For each student, use **In** / **Out** to record a check-in or check-out.
  - Recording a check-in/out **instantly notifies that child's parents**.
  - The summary cards show Present / Late / Absent for the class; the roster
    shows each student's status and check-in/out times.
- A check-in before **08:00** counts as *present*; after **08:00** as *late*
  (school local time).

## 3. Class management
- **Sidebar → My Classes** — your classes, subjects, and today's timetable.
- Manage rosters and records via **Education → Students / Classes / Subjects**.
- **Lesson Planner** (Education → Lesson Planner) to prepare and publish lessons.
- **eClassroom** for assignments and submissions; **CBT** for computer-based tests.

## 4. Results
- **Gradebook** (Education → Gradebook) and **Exams & Results** to enter scores.
- **CBT** to create tests, add questions, and review attempts.
- **Report Cards** to review a student's term report.

## 5. Notifications
- The **Notifications** item shows recent school activity relevant to you.
- Use **Messenger** and the **News Feed** to communicate with staff and post
  class updates.
- Parents automatically receive attendance alerts whenever you record a
  check-in/out — no extra step needed.

## 6. Tips
- You can only see and act on **your** classes and students.
- Record attendance early in the day so parents get timely arrival alerts.
- Save lesson plans as drafts, then publish when ready.
